Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed Potatoes are a regular sighting during holiday meals, though they make for a quick and easy homemade meal for any occasion. Loaded with potatoes, garlic, milk, butter, and salt and pepper to taste, mashed potatoes are a lovely savory, creamy, and filling dish to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and garlic, lower heat to medium, and simmer until the potatoes are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. When the potatoes are almost finished, heat milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until the butter is melted.
  3. Drain potatoes and return to the pot. Slowly add warm milk mixture, blending it in with a potato masher or electric mixer until potatoes are smooth and creamy. Season with salt and pepper.
